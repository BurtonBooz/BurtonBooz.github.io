JOYWAVE

"Possession"

The 3rd Studio Album

Promotional media produced for the release of Joywave's third studio album Possession. This included the 12-episode miniseries "Tales of Possession" narrated by lead singer Daniel Armbruster, exploring the mythology and concepts underpinning the 12 tracks on the album. Additionally, a lyric video was created for the song "Half Your Age".

Role: Illustration, animation, typography.

Directed by Jared Asher Harris
Produced by Sixtwentysix
