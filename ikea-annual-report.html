IKEA - Yearly Summary

Proposed re-design of the IKEA Yearly Summary from FY16. Focus is placed on the profiles and graphic shapes inherent in IKEA products, emphasizing the democratized design principles and utilitarian nature of the company.

Created for the Typography 3 class taught by Jason Mannix at the Maryland Institute College of Art
